# Contributing

Thanks for helping improve Awesome OPC.

## What Fits

Good additions should be directly useful for understanding or building AI-enabled one-person companies:

- agentic AI and LLM agent surveys
- multi-agent coordination and role-based agent teams
- GUI, browser, OS, and computer-use agents
- AI research automation and knowledge-work automation
- agent security, permissions, auditability, and governance
- solopreneur, nanocorp, and small-business reports
- legal One Person Company or single-shareholder company studies

## Entry Format

Please use the existing table style:

```markdown
| YYYY/MM | [Title](https://paper-or-resource-url) | One concise sentence explaining why it matters for OPC. | [Github](https://github.com/example/repo) |
```

Use `-` in the `Code` column when no public code or project page is available.

## Curation Rules

- Prefer surveys, systematic reviews, benchmark papers, open-source projects, and high-signal industry reports.
- Keep descriptions short and specific.
- Do not add generic AI tools unless they clearly support agentic company automation.
- For legal and business resources, make clear whether the item is about legal OPC structure, solopreneur markets, or agentic automation.
- Use stable links whenever possible: arXiv, publisher pages, official project pages, GitHub repositories, or official reports.

## Pull Requests

Please include:

- the category where the item belongs
- why the item is relevant to OPC
- whether code, data, or a project page is available

Small focused PRs are easier to review.
